UI element: Overlay Card Modal. Canvas 512x320 px. 9-slice. Used for Victory/Defeat/Reward.

The largest UI surface in the game — a full-screen-width modal card that appears
over the battle background for victory results, defeat, and loot reveal moments.
It must feel significant without being harsh. Per art-bible §2.4 (Victory) and §2.5
(Defeat): Victory warms and lifts; Defeat cools and drains.

GENERATE TWO VARIANTS:

VARIANT 1 — VICTORY CARD (overlay_card_victory.png):
The reward moment — warm, resolved, luminous. Per §2.4: "warm exhale, satisfaction with
ownership." The card should feel like a trophy case opening.

- OUTER EDGE: Reward Gold #E8B820 — 2px border (the achievement moment uses gold)
- CHAMFERED CORNERS: 45-degree cuts, ~20px diagonal (large card, large chamfer)
- BODY FILL: C-2 Mid Panel #2C3340 (standard panel fill — the warmth comes from the
  border and any content placed inside, not from an orange body)
- INNER BORDER: 1px Reward Gold #E8B820 at 1px inset from outer edge
  (double-gold-line frame — premium feel)
- SOFT INNER GLOW: 8px inner glow at ~15% opacity in Reward Gold #E8B820, bleeding
  inward from the border — the "warm luminous" quality of victory
- TOP ACCENT: 2px Reward Gold line along the top inner edge (the card reads as
  lit from above — the trophy moment has a soft top-light)

VARIANT 2 — DEFEAT CARD (overlay_card_defeat.png):
The analytical moment — cool, still, temporary. Per §2.5: "clear-eyed sting of a
disproved hypothesis — reset, not shame." Not harsh. Weighted but not punishing.

- OUTER EDGE: C-5 Divider #4B5668 — 2px (no gold, no warmth)
- CHAMFERED CORNERS: same 20px diagonal geometry
- BODY FILL: C-1 HUD Dark #1E2229 (drops to the darkest surface — the scene flattens)
- INNER BORDER: 1px C-5 #4B5668 at inset
- SOFT INNER GLOW: 8px inner glow at ~10% opacity in Circuit Teal #2B6E68 (cool,
  analytical tone — not red punishment, just the cool drain of a failed experiment)
- TOP ACCENT: none (no bevel highlight — the defeat card is flat, no uplift)

SHARED ANATOMY (both variants):
- No rounded corners on either variant
- No content baked in (text, icons, XP numbers all placed by UI programmer)
- A subtle texture in the body fill area: very faint (3%) noise suggesting a
  machined/industrial surface — the card feels solid, not empty
- The 24px margins contain the border + chamfer geometry cleanly

GREYSCALE TEST: Victory card has a bright border (gold = high luminance) with a
warm inner glow. Defeat card has a mid-grey border and darker body. Both read as
distinct states in greyscale by border luminance contrast.

NEGATIVE: NO ROUNDED CORNERS. No harsh red on the defeat card (losses are not
punishment — cool teal, not anger-red). No Danger Red #CC3020 on defeat card.
No gradient body fill. No decorative content in the stretch zone. No photoreal.
