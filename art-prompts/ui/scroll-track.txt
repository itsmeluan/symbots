UI element: Scrollbar Track + Grabber. Vertical scrollbar for inventory and catalog panels.
Track: 16x256 px 9-slice. Grabber: 16x48 px fixed.

TRACK ANATOMY (16x256 px, 9-slice vertical):
A narrow vertical channel that the grabber slides within. The track is recessed —
it reads as a groove cut into the panel surface.

- Width: 16px total; the visible track is 8px centered (4px left margin, 4px right)
- Track fill: C-1 HUD Dark #1E2229 (recessed groove)
- Track edges (L+R): 1px C-5 Divider #4B5668 on each side of the groove
- Track end caps (top/bottom, 12px each): rounded ends for the groove terminus
  EXCEPTION TO CHAMFER RULE: scrollbar track ends may use small radius (4px) because
  the track is a SLOT (a contained groove), not a panel or button. This is the
  only rounded element in the UI. Per art-bible §3.6: "chamfer = interactive surfaces."
  The scrollbar track is not an interactive surface — it is a channel.
- Stretch zone (vertical center): flat C-1 fill flanked by C-5 edge lines

GRABBER ANATOMY (16x48 px, fixed):
The draggable scroll handle. This IS an interactive surface — it uses chamfered corners.

- OUTER EDGE: 1px C-5 Divider #4B5668
- GRABBER BODY: C-3 Interactive Surface #3A4455
- CHAMFERED CORNERS: 45-degree cuts at all four corners, ~4px diagonal
  (proportional to the small 16x48 canvas)
- CENTER GRIP DETAIL: 3 horizontal 1px lines centered vertically on the grabber,
  spaced 3px apart, in C-5 #4B5668 — the classic "grip lines" that communicate
  "drag me." These must be visible at the 16x48 display size.
- HOVER STATE: grabber body brightens to C-2 #2C3340; grip lines lighten

GREYSCALE TEST: Track reads as a dark recessed groove; grabber reads as a lighter
raised handle sitting within the groove. Clear figure/ground.

NEGATIVE: NO ROUNDED CORNERS on the grabber. NO organic curves on the grabber.
The track may have slight end-cap radius (scrollbar exception above). No photoreal.
No gradient fill. No glow.
