UI element: Standard Panel Frame. Canvas 256x256 px. 9-slice panel background.

The foundational panel background for all HUD cards, info boxes, and UI containers
in Symbots. Unlike the hud-log-panel-frame (which is a transparent-center ring),
this panel has a SOLID filled background — it is the full opaque surface that
UI content sits on top of.

ANATOMY:
1. OUTER EDGE: 1px C-5 Divider #4B5668 — the outermost line
2. CHAMFERED CORNERS: 45-degree cuts at all four corners, ~14px diagonal
   (the chamfer fits within the 16px margin cleanly — the cut resolves inside the margin)
3. BODY FILL: C-2 Mid Panel #2C3340 — the main surface color
   This is the default "mid" register — above the HUD Dark base plate but below
   the Interactive surface. Content (text, icons) placed on this reads at good contrast.
4. INNER BORDER: 1px C-5 #4B5668 at 1px inset from the outer edge —
   a double-line frame effect (outer + inner edge) that gives the panel visual depth
5. TOP EDGE ACCENT: 1px C-5 #4B5668 at the very top inner edge, slightly lighter
   variant — very subtle bevel implying top-down light source (workshop-bench-lamp metaphor)

9-SLICE CENTER (the stretchable zone):
Flat C-2 #2C3340 fill, zero decoration. The center must tile without artifacts.

USES: Enemy card background, move-panel container, Workshop stat display background,
any info box that needs to present content on a mid-grey surface.

GREYSCALE TEST: The panel reads as a flat dark-grey rectangle with a subtle border
and four distinct chamfered corners. The corners are the signature element.

NEGATIVE: NO ROUNDED CORNERS. No gradient fill in the center zone. No glow. No
organic curves. No tiling decoration in the stretch zone. No photoreal.
