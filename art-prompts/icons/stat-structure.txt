Icon: STRUCTURE stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Structure is the bot's HP — the primary survivability stat. When Structure reaches
zero the bot is defeated. The icon must communicate: integrity, durability, the body
holding together.

ICON DESIGN: A structural plate / armored panel motif:
- A thick rectangular or hexagonal plate shape (the silhouette of a single armor
  panel — the bot's body plating). Wide, solid, protective.
- A visible panel-line or seam across the face (one horizontal or diagonal line
  across the plate surface) — communicates "engineered plating," not a blank disc.
- Optionally: a small crack or stress mark at one corner at 20% of full stroke weight
  (very faint — implies "this is what you're protecting") but keep this subtle.
- 2px outline weight for the outer plate shape, 1px for the interior seam.
- Centered, bold, reads as "armor/body" at 16x16 minimum.

VISUAL LOGIC: Structure is the resource that maps to the Structure bar (green fill).
The icon's plate-shape echoes the plating language of the CHASSIS slot —
"the body holding together" is the core read.

NEGATIVE: No heart shape (this is a machine game — heart = organic health, not
structure). No HP-cross symbol. No color. No element glyph. No photoreal.
