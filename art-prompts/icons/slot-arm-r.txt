Icon: ARMS (Right) slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Mirror of slot-arm-l.png. Identical arm form, mirrored to point RIGHT.
In practice the UI programmer may simply flip slot-arm-l.png horizontally —
but a dedicated right-arm icon is provided for clarity in context menus and
inventory where left/right distinction may matter.

ICON DESIGN: Same as ARMS (Left) but mirrored:
- The arm extends to the RIGHT overall
- Upper arm, elbow, forearm, hand/wrist face
- Mount node at the shoulder (right side)
- 2px outline, same proportions as the left arm icon

NEGATIVE: Same as slot-arm-l. No weapon. No chassis. No color. No element glyph.
