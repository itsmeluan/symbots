Icon: HEAD slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, flat icon design.

A small symbolic icon representing the HEAD equipment slot in the Workshop,
inventory, and combat target-picker UIs. Per art-bible §3.2: HEAD = compact,
elevated, with a dominant horizontal sensor feature (visor band / lens cluster).

This icon is a GLYPH (monochrome silhouette/outline) — it communicates slot type
through shape alone. It renders in C-6 UI White #E8E8E8 on a C-1 HUD Dark #1E2229
plate (placed by UI programmer). The sprite should be drawn as white on transparent.

ICON DESIGN: A stylized helmet/sensor head form:
- A compact rounded-top form (the head shape — widest at the mid, slight taper top)
- A dominant horizontal visor/sensor bar across the face (the mandatory HEAD feature)
- The visor bar is the single most recognizable element — at 32x32 it must be clear
- Simple, bold, readable at 16x16 if scaled down (minimum legibility target)
- Flat, 2px outline weight, minimal interior detail

The icon should suggest: sensor crest, elevated, horizontal optic sweep.

GREYSCALE: It IS greyscale — white on transparent. Works as a monochrome overlay.

NEGATIVE: No color fills. No element glyph (that is a separate icon). No complex
mechanical detail. No robot body — just the HEAD zone silhouette. No photoreal.
