Icon: ENERGY POWER stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Energy Power determines the damage of energy-based attacks (Volt and Thermal elements).
The icon must communicate: projected energy, a discharged burst, ranged force.

ICON DESIGN: An energy burst / projected bolt motif:
- A compact starburst or radial burst shape — angular rays (4-6 rays) projecting
  outward from a small center point. NOT a circle: the center is a small diamond
  or square, and the rays are straight angular lines of varying length
  (longest rays in the cardinal directions, shorter rays between them).
- The rays are straight and hard-edged (not curved, not flame-like — this is
  mechanical energy, not organic fire).
- Alternatively: a compact forward-facing bolt/emitter shape — a small circle
  (the emitter) with 3-4 straight lines projecting forward-right in a focused
  burst pattern (like a laser or arc discharge in the forward direction).
- 2px center shape, 1-2px ray/bolt lines.

DISTINCTION FROM PHYSICAL POWER: Energy Power has NO contact point — energy
is projected outward. The star/burst radiating from center (or forward-burst from
emitter) reads as "projected force, not contact force."

DISTINCTION FROM ELEMENT GLYPHS: This is a generic energy stat icon, not
affiliated with any specific element (Volt/Thermal/Kinetic). It must NOT look like
the Lightning Fork (Volt) or Flame Chevron (Thermal). Use a radial burst or
simple multi-ray motif that reads as "energy output" without implying a specific element.

NEGATIVE: No Lightning Fork shape (Volt glyph). No upward Vs (Thermal glyph).
No concentric circles (Kinetic / Impact Ring glyph). No color. No organic flame.
No contact/impact lines. No photoreal.
