Icon: TARGETING stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Targeting affects accuracy and the ability to hit specific break regions.
Higher targeting = more reliable hits on targeted regions. The icon must
communicate: precision, aim, hitting a specific point.

ICON DESIGN: A precision reticle / crosshair motif — but angular:
- A small diamond or square in the CENTER (the target point).
- Four short straight lines extending from the center diamond outward in the
  four cardinal directions (up, down, left, right) — like a crosshair's tick marks.
  These are SHORT (about 4-5px each) and do NOT connect to the canvas edge.
  They terminate with a small perpendicular end cap (like a surveying reticle).
- The gap between the center diamond and the tick lines is visible — the classic
  "open" reticle with a center dot and separated tick marks.
- 2px center diamond, 1-2px tick lines.

DISTINCTION FROM hud-target-reticle.txt (the 128x128 HUD overlay):
That asset is a large chamfered RECTANGLE used as a targeting overlay in combat.
This icon is a small 32x32 CROSSHAIR GLYPH used to represent the Targeting STAT
in the Workshop and UI. They serve different purposes and must not be confused.

The crosshair motif is the clearest "accuracy/aim" read. The "open" reticle
(center point with separated ticks, not connected lines) is the precision signal.

NEGATIVE: No circular outer ring (the Impact Ring / Kinetic element glyph uses
concentric circles — avoid confusion by keeping this icon non-circular in its
outer structure). No color. No element glyph. No filled solid disc. No photoreal.
