Icon: CHIPSET slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The CHIPSET slot icon. CHIPSET = embedded processing/intelligence module.
Per art-bible §3.3: flush-embedded in CHASSIS, no silhouette protrusion. The icon
communicates the electronic/processing nature of this slot.

ICON DESIGN: A circuit board / chip form:
- A small square or rectangular chip form (smaller than the CHASSIS icon — compact)
- Circuit trace lines radiating from the chip body at its corners or edges
  (like PCB trace lines extending outward — the Boltwell circuit-board vocabulary
  but in generic glyph form)
- A small dot or square in the center of the chip body (processor die or core element)
- The circuit traces communicate "electronic / processing" at a glance
- 2px outline, trace lines at 1px, clean and technical

The icon communicates: "logic. Intelligence. Embedded processing module."

NEGATIVE: No limb shape. No weapon. No chassis body mass. No color.
No complex detail beyond circuit traces. No photoreal.
