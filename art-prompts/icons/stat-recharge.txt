Icon: RECHARGE stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Recharge is the rate at which Energy replenishes each turn. Higher Recharge =
recover Energy faster. The icon must communicate: flowing replenishment,
energy returning, regeneration rate.

ICON DESIGN: A cell-plus-upward-arrow motif:
- A compact battery/cell form (smaller than the Energy Capacity icon — roughly
  the lower half of the canvas, 14x18 px) with terminal tabs at the top.
- A bold UPWARD-POINTING ARROW above or overlapping the top of the cell —
  the arrow exits from the cell's positive terminal pointing upward, communicating
  "energy flowing INTO the cell from above / energy regenerating."
- The arrow is 2-3px wide, pointed head, straight vertical shaft.
- The cell and arrow together occupy the canvas — the arrow is approximately
  the same visual weight as the cell body.
- 2px outline on both the cell and the arrow shaft/head.

ALTERNATIVE arrangement: A small cell on the left, a rightward circular cycle
arrow on the right (the refresh/cycle symbol — a circular arrow indicating
"this replenishes on a cycle"). If the circular arrow reads as a cycle/refresh
at 32x32, use this as the alternative. The circular arrow should be angular
(squared corners, not smoothly rounded).

DISTINCTION FROM ENERGY CAPACITY: Capacity shows a FULL cell (the pool size).
Recharge shows a cell WITH AN ARROW (the flow/rate of refilling). The arrow
is the key differentiator — the upward motion communicates "rate of return."

NEGATIVE: No downward arrow (that would communicate draining, not recharging).
No circular concentric rings (Kinetic Impact Ring). No color. No element glyph.
No curved organic shapes. No photoreal.
