Icon: ENERGY_CELL slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The ENERGY_CELL slot icon. ENERGY_CELL = the stored-power module that fuels the bot's
energy resource. Flush-embedded in the CHASSIS like CHIPSET — no silhouette protrusion.
The icon must communicate: stored power, battery, charge reservoir.

ICON DESIGN: A battery / capacitor cell form:
- A compact rectangular or slightly rounded-rectangular cell body (taller than wide —
  the classic battery proportions, oriented vertically to distinguish it from CHIPSET)
- Terminal contacts at the TOP: one or two small rectangular tabs protruding from the
  top edge (positive/negative terminal convention — the clearest "battery" read)
- A horizontal fill-level indicator line roughly 2/3 of the way up the cell body
  (a charge level stripe — communicates "this holds charge")
- 2px outline for the body, 1px for the terminal tabs and fill line
- Simple, bold, immediately readable as "battery" at 16x16 minimum

DISTINCTION FROM CHIPSET (slot-chipset.txt): CHIPSET is a horizontal PCB/chip form
with circuit traces radiating outward. ENERGY_CELL is a vertical battery form with
terminal tabs at the top. The orientation and terminal tabs make them unmistakable.

The icon communicates: "stored power. Charge reservoir. Energy capacity."

NEGATIVE: No circuit trace lines (that is CHIPSET). No horizontal orientation as
primary form. No color. No element glyph. No complex detail. No photoreal.
