Icon: MOBILITY stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Mobility affects turn order and evasion. Higher mobility = acts sooner and
potentially avoids some attacks. The icon must communicate: speed, agility,
turn priority, being-first.

ICON DESIGN: A forward-movement arrow / speed boot motif:
- A bold rightward-pointing arrow — the clearest "speed/forward" glyph at 32x32.
  The arrow is angular with a sharp triangular head (not rounded) and a thick
  rectangular tail. The arrow fills the canvas to be as bold and readable as possible.
- Optional refinement: two parallel forward arrows (one slightly behind the other,
  both pointing right) — the "double chevron" speed read. Only if both arrows
  read clearly at 32x32; if it gets busy, use the single bold arrow.
- The arrow(s) point RIGHT (direction the combatant "moves forward" in turn order).
- 2-3px stroke weight on the arrow to be bold and readable.

ALTERNATIVE if arrows feel too generic: A mechanical boot/foot silhouette with
a forward-sweep motion line — the LEGS slot but in motion. A single angular
boot/tread shape with 2-3 parallel speed lines trailing behind it to the left.

DISTINCTION FROM STAT ICONS: Mobility is the only directional stat icon (it has
a clear rightward pointing direction). All other stat icons are symmetrical or
orientation-neutral.

NEGATIVE: No circular form (that is Kinetic Impact Ring). No energy burst (Energy Power).
No color. No element glyph. No photoreal. No curved or organic shapes.
