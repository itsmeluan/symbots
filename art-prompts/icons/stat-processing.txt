Icon: PROCESSING stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Processing affects how quickly the bot computes actions and may govern
synergy evaluation speed or AI response time. The icon must communicate:
computation, data processing, intelligence throughput.

ICON DESIGN: A processor node / circuit-compute motif:
- A small square or octagonal chip shape in the center (the processor die —
  slightly more complex than the CHIPSET slot glyph).
- Short connector traces radiating from the chip's FOUR CORNERS diagonally
  outward (not the cardinal directions — diagonal traces distinguish Processing
  from Targeting which uses cardinal-direction ticks).
- Each diagonal trace terminates in a small square connector pad.
- The result: a chip with 4 diagonal connections to 4 corner pads — a classic
  PCB "component with traces" silhouette.
- 2px chip outline, 1px trace lines, 2x2 px corner pads.

DISTINCTION FROM CHIPSET SLOT GLYPH (slot-chipset.txt): The CHIPSET slot glyph
has traces radiating from the chip edges/sides in multiple directions and represents
the slot category. The PROCESSING stat glyph has only 4 diagonal corner traces
and represents the speed/throughput of computation. Shape is similar but the
diagonal-corner-only trace pattern differentiates at 32x32.

NEGATIVE: No cardinal-direction ticks (that is Targeting). No color.
No element glyph. No battery terminals (that is Energy Cell). No photoreal.
