Icon: WEAPON slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The WEAPON slot icon. WEAPON = the only slot that extends FORWARD (toward the target).
Per art-bible §3.3: "The only slot expected to break the CHASSIS silhouette boundary
outward (forward/horizontal); the weapon must 'point.'"

ICON DESIGN: An abstract weapon-forward form:
- A bold horizontal or slightly angled forward element — the weapon pointing right
  (toward the enemy, which is on the right side of the combat screen by convention)
- A barrel/shaft running left-to-right (horizontal)
- A slightly wider/emphasized "business end" at the right (the emitter/tip/head —
  the part that points toward the target)
- A narrower mount end at the left (where it connects to the ARM)
- 2px outline, simple interior, bold and direct

The icon communicates: "weapon. It points. The threat comes from this."

NEGATIVE: No limb shape. No chassis shape. No melee weapon angled downward in
this icon (generic slot glyph uses the forward-horizontal reading). No color.
No element glyph. No complex mechanical detail. No photoreal.
