Icon: PHYSICAL POWER stat glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

Physical Power determines the damage of Kinetic (physical) attacks. The icon
must communicate: striking force, impact, mechanical brute strength.

ICON DESIGN: A clenched fist / impact motif — but mechanized:
- A mechanical fist or gauntlet silhouette: a compact boxy fist shape with
  angular knuckle geometry (not an organic human fist — a mechanical arm's
  terminal end in a striking position, like the forearm-and-fist of the ARMS slot).
- The fist is angled forward-and-slightly-down (the melee strike angle, consistent
  with art-bible WEAPON angle for melee = down-and-inward).
- Knuckle plates visible: 3-4 rectangular knuckle segments across the top of the fist
  (Ironclad-style — flat geometric plates).
- A small impact starburst or stress lines radiating from the knuckle face
  (1-2 short straight lines, NOT a circular starburst — keep it angular).
- 2px fist outline, 1px knuckle lines and impact marks.

ALTERNATIVE (if fist reads unclear at 32x32): A heavy hammer-strike silhouette —
a rectangular hammer head making contact with a flat surface, with 2-3 angular
impact lines radiating from the contact point. Same "force on impact" read.

DISTINCTION FROM ENERGY POWER: Physical Power = contact/impact (fist/hammer,
mechanical contact). Energy Power = projected energy (bolt/burst, no physical contact).
The presence or absence of a direct contact motif is the differentiator.

NEGATIVE: No energy glow or projected blast (that is Energy Power). No color.
No element glyph (this is a stat icon, not an element marker). No organic fist
(angular mechanical form). No photoreal.
