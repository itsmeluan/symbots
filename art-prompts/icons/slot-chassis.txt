Icon: CHASSIS slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The CHASSIS slot icon. CHASSIS = the torso/body plate — always the widest element,
the mass anchor of the bot. The icon must communicate: torso/body/core housing.

ICON DESIGN: A stylized broad torso form:
- Wide rectangular or trapezoidal shape (wider than tall, or equally proportioned)
- Shoulder notches at the upper-left and upper-right corners where ARMS would attach
  (two small rectangular notches cut from the upper corners — attachment grammar
  visualized in the icon)
- A small circular or diamond void in the center of the chest face (implied CORE
  socket — this is the CHASSIS, which houses the CORE)
- Lower edge is straight (where LEGS would attach below)
- Bold outline, simple interior, 2px line weight

The icon communicates: "the body. The widest part. The anchor."

NEGATIVE: No HEAD or LEGS elements in this icon. No weapon protrusion. No color.
No element glyph. No complex panel-line detail. No photoreal.
