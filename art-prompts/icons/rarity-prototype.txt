Icon: PROTOTYPE rarity frame. Canvas 72x72 px. Frame ring, transparent center.

Per art-bible §4.4: Prototype = "Stochastic flicker <3 Hz + chromatic-aberration shimmer.
Irregular animated border + warning mark. Greyscale read: animated border."
Per §4.4: "Flash-safety: Prototype flicker is capped <3 Hz per accessibility-requirements.md
(photosensitivity floor). The shimmer is amplitude-modulated, not a hard on/off strobe."

This is a STILL FRAME of what the animated border looks like at mid-flicker.
The animation is applied in-engine — this sprite captures the visual character.

FRAME ANATOMY (still frame at mid-flicker):
- OUTER DIMENSIONS: 72x72 px
- CENTER TRANSPARENT ZONE: 62x62 px (alpha = 0)
- BORDER CHARACTER — IRREGULAR AND SLIGHTLY UNSTABLE:
  The Prototype border is NOT a perfectly clean geometric border like Common/Rare/Boss.
  It is a slightly irregular, glitching border — as if the frame itself is unstable:
  - BASE BORDER: 2px in C-6 UI White #E8E8E8, but with slight pixel-level
    irregularity — some segments 1px, some 2px, occasional 1px gap where the border
    seems to momentarily break (the "glitch" quality of Prototype instability)
  - CHAMFERED CORNERS: present but slightly imprecise — the corner angles are close
    to 45 degrees but not perfectly regular (one corner may be 1px off)
  - CHROMATIC ABERRATION SHIMMER: a 1-2px color-fringe on the outer border edge —
    a cyan fringe (#2FE8E8 at 40%) offset 1px to the left of the border, and a
    red-magenta fringe (#CC2060 approximation at 30%) offset 1px to the right.
    This is the "chromatic shimmer" captured as a still color-aberration fringe.
  - No inner borders (unlike Boss/Rare — the Prototype's instability makes it
    visually complex but not structured like the lower-tier triple-border)
- WARNING MARK: a ⚠ triangle-warning symbol at the lower-right corner of the frame.
  Size: 8x8 px. Color: C-6 UI White #E8E8E8. Non-color channel for Prototype tier.
- AMPLITUDE-MODULATED GLOW: in this still frame, a soft element-colored glow of
  ~40% opacity around the border (mid-flicker amplitude state — not full brightness,
  not zero). The glow bleeds outward 3px and has a slight chromatic color-split.

GREYSCALE READ: An IRREGULAR/IMPRECISE border (distinguishable from the clean-line
borders of Common/Rare/Boss purely by its irregular pixel behavior) + a warning triangle.
In greyscale the animation is not readable, but the warning mark IS — making Prototype
identifiable even in non-animated still frame.

NEGATIVE: No hard-strobe on/off effect (flicker must be amplitude-modulated per a11y).
No four clean borders (this is irregular, not structured). NO ROUNDED CORNERS on the
base geometry (the irregularity is pixel-level imprecision, not rounding). No photoreal.
No label text.
