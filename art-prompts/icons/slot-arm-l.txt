Icon: ARMS (Left) slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The left ARM slot icon. ARMS = extend laterally from CHASSIS, the power limbs.
At 32x32 this is an arm/limb silhouette pointing left (the left arm, viewed
from front-facing perspective — arm extends to the player's right visually = LEFT arm
of the bot). For simplicity, generate as a LEFT-POINTING arm shape.

ICON DESIGN: A simplified mechanical arm form:
- An upper arm rectangular segment (shoulder-to-elbow)
- An elbow joint implied (slight angle change)
- A forearm segment tapering slightly toward the hand end
- A flat or slightly-angled hand/wrist face (where WEAPON would attach)
- The arm points LEFT overall (it is the left arm)
- A mount node implied at the shoulder end (where it attaches to CHASSIS)
- 2px outline weight, no complex interior detail

The icon communicates: "limb, extending sideways, left side."

NEGATIVE: No WEAPON attached (that is a separate slot). No CHASSIS body included.
No color. No element glyph. No complex mechanical detail. No photoreal.
