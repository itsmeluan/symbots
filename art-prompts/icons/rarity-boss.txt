Icon: BOSS-GRADE rarity frame. Canvas 72x72 px. Frame ring, transparent center.

Per art-bible §4.4: Boss-Grade = "Triple border + star + accent band. Steady radiant
glow (~60%) + shader edge. Greyscale read: 3 borders."

FRAME ANATOMY:
- OUTER DIMENSIONS: 72x72 px
- CENTER TRANSPARENT ZONE: 60x60 px (alpha = 0)
- BORDER STRUCTURE — THREE DISTINCT BORDERS:
  - OUTERMOST BORDER: 2px, Reward Gold #E8B820 — the gold treatment signals
    "exceptional item" (Reward Gold semantic, art-bible §4.3 star/trophy association)
  - MIDDLE BORDER: 1px, C-6 UI White #E8E8E8 at 1px gap from the outer border
  - INNER BORDER: 1px, C-5 Divider #4B5668 at 1px gap from the middle border
  - Total border stack depth: ~7px from outer edge to inner edge of the stack
- CHAMFERED CORNERS: 45-degree cuts at all four corners, ~5px diagonal
- STAR MARK: a ★ star shape placed at the top-center or lower-right of the frame.
  Size: approximately 8x8 px. Filled Reward Gold #E8B820. This is the non-color
  Boss-Grade marker — larger than the Rare diamond pip, the star shape is distinct.
- ACCENT BAND: a thin 1px line of Reward Gold #E8B820 running along the top inner
  edge of the frame (just inside the inner border) — "accent band" per §4.4.
- STEADY RADIANT GLOW: ~60% opacity element-colored glow bleeding outward from the
  outermost border (the engine applies the element color at runtime; in this generic
  frame sprite the glow is a warm gold-tinted radiance from the outer gold border).
  The glow is 4-5px wide outward from the border, falling off from 60% to 0%.

GREYSCALE READ: THREE distinct border lines (triple border) + the star mark.
The border count (3 vs Rare's 2 vs Common's 1) is the greyscale rarity signal.
The star is larger and more prominent than the Rare diamond pip.

NEGATIVE: No four borders (Prototype has animated/irregular, not four clean ones).
No animated elements in this still sprite. NO ROUNDED CORNERS. No organic curves.
No label text. No photoreal.
