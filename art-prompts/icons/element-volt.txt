Icon: VOLT element glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Colored icon on transparent background.

The canonical Volt element glyph used on the element badge (target-picker PG-06),
part icons, move cards, and status effect indicators throughout the game.
Per art-bible §4.2: the Volt glyph is the Lightning Fork — "bifurcated diagonal bolt."

GLYPH DESIGN — LIGHTNING FORK:
A diagonal lightning bolt that BIFURCATES (splits into two) at its lower end.
The canonical shape:
- Main bolt: a thick diagonal stroke running from upper-left to center-right,
  with a sharp angular zig-zag (the lightning kink — one angular bend in the middle)
- Fork: at the lower end of the bolt, the line splits into TWO diverging tips
  (lower-left and lower-right directions) — the "fork" that distinguishes this from
  a plain lightning bolt
- The fork split is the most important feature — it makes the Volt glyph unique
  and distinguishes it from a generic lightning icon
- Bold, 3px stroke minimum, clean hard edges (no soft brush strokes)
- Centered within the 32x32 canvas with ~3px margin on all sides

COLOR: Volt Cyan #2FE8E8 on transparent background.
Render as a solid filled glyph (not outline only — solid cyan shape).

VERIFIED CONTRAST ON C-1 #1E2229 DARK PLATE: Volt cyan ≈ 7.2:1 (per art-bible §4.5).
The glyph always renders on a C-1 plate in the game — design it to read clearly there.

GREYSCALE TEST: The Lightning Fork shape reads as a bifurcated lightning bolt
in greyscale. The bifurcation (the fork) is the non-color shape channel.

NEGATIVE: No plain single-tip lightning bolt (must bifurcate). No Thermal Flame
Chevron shape (different glyph). No circle (that is Kinetic). No soft edges.
No animated shimmer in this still icon. No photoreal.
