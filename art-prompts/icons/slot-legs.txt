Icon: LEGS slot glyph. Canvas 32x32 px. Monochrome, white on transparent.

The LEGS slot icon. LEGS = ground-contact, the bot's stance foundation, always
the lowest element. The icon must communicate: legs, ground, stance, foundation.

ICON DESIGN: A stylized bipedal stance form — viewed from front:
- Two leg columns side by side, symmetric
- Each leg has an upper section (thigh) and a lower section (shin/foot)
- Foot contact implied at the bottom (the lowest element — flat ground-contact feet)
- Mount connection implied at the top of each leg (where they join the CHASSIS)
- Overall shape: wider at the hip connection, narrowing or parallel down to the feet
- Feet spread slightly outward (planted stance — communicates "foundation")
- 2px outline, minimal interior detail, readable at 16x16

The icon communicates: "stance. Ground contact. Foundation of the bot."

NEGATIVE: No CHASSIS body above the hip mount. No head. No arms. No color.
No element glyph. No complex mechanical detail. No photoreal.
