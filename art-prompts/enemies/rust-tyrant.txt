Enemy Symbot: Rust Tyrant. BOSS. Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 side-view, low-camera angle.

CONCEPT: "The Rustcrawlers' apex — a scrap-forged warlord that grew by devouring its own
kind." This is the Rustcrawler lineage taken to its extreme — a boss that grew by
consuming and absorbing other Scrapjaw machines. It should visually read as if it is
MADE OF other machines: parts of smaller bots incorporated into its body, welded over
with new mass, layers upon layers of scavenged additions. The visual history of its
growth is readable on its body.

BOSS-GRADE VISUAL TREATMENT (art-bible §4.4 / §5.4):
- Significantly larger mass than any common enemy — fills the canvas more fully
- Steady radiant element-color glow (silver-shift Kinetic) along the model edge
- Boss-Grade implies triple-border + star in any UI framing, but the model itself
  reads as boss through SCALE and mass, not just glow
- All three break regions must be distinct targetable silhouette zones

SILHOUETTE DESCRIPTION:
Massive, asymmetric, layered body plan. The Rust Tyrant is a Scrapjaw build taken to
extremes — every surface shows layers of absorbed/welded-on parts from consumed enemies.
Core silhouette elements:
- ARMS (region_fraction 35%): the most prominent feature — one arm is much larger than
  the other (dominant "crusher claw" — the largest single appendage, extending wide
  and forward; this is what the player needs to break first). The crusher claw is the
  widest element at the ARM zone, made from welded-together salvaged claws and hooks.
  The smaller ARM still exists but is secondary.
- HEAD (region_fraction 25%): elevated sensor crest with multiple stacked optical
  elements (absorbed sensor units from other bots, creating an asymmetric multi-eye
  cluster). Still reads as HEAD by position (topmost elevated element) but feels
  accumulated rather than designed. A horizontal composite sensor sweep.
- CORE (region_fraction 40%): the CORE housing in the CHASSIS center is the biggest
  break pool. The CHASSIS around it looks layered — outer armor shells stacked on an
  inner chassis, with the core housing at the center implying "the original machine
  inside all this growth."

SCRAPJAW MANUFACTURER FINISH AMPLIFIED: All of the Scrapjaw vocabulary, but accumulated
over time. Multiple overlapping diagonal slash panel-line generations (new welds over old).
Quick-release clasps that have been welded permanently shut. Asymmetry in every dimension.
Harvest Crimson #B33020 faction paint in patches, partially obscured by new welds.
Surface tells the story of construction-by-consumption.

KINETIC ELEMENT ACCENT: Silver-shift #D8DDE6 on the crusher claw impact surfaces
(heavy chrome polished facing — the most dangerous part shines). Impact Ring glyph on
the crusher claw face. Boss-Grade steady radiant silver glow along the outer model edge.
Chrome finish on the claw is the shiniest surface in the scene (Kinetic boss reads
power through polished impact metal).

IDLE STANCE: Wide stance, weight low and forward, crusher claw raised and angled inward
(ready to crush — melee WEAPON angle = down-and-inward). HEAD sensor array scanning
menacingly. The smaller ARM is at side. Reads: "I have been here before. You have not."
Massive bottom-heaviness. Intimidating, not agile.

WORLD PALETTE BASE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 dominant chassis mass; Harvest Crimson
#B33020 on faction paint patches; Alloy Ochre #C49A35 on rust and weathering; Bone White
on structural frame and crusher claw chrome.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64 greyscale — CRUSHER CLAW ARM (dominant wide lateral element),
elevated HEAD (multi-sensor elevated crest), and massive CHASSIS BODY (with implied
CORE housing at center) must each read as distinct targetable zones by shape and position.
The boss must be unmistakably larger and more complex than any common enemy.

NEGATIVE: No clean symmetric build (this is deliberately asymmetric and accumulated).
No Boltwell or Ironclad finish (pure Scrapjaw — scavenged). No exposed glowing CORE
sphere. No facial expressions. No photoreal. No painterly gradients. Must be visually
larger than all common enemies. No rounded UI.
