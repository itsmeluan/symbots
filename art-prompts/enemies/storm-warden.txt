Enemy Symbot: Storm Warden. BOSS. Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 side-view, low-camera angle.

CONCEPT: "A Boltwell command unit crackling with stormlight. Its lance was never meant
to leave the cradle." A flagship machine — built to command and never deployed as a
frontline unit until now. It carries the Storm Lance, a weapon of extraordinary power
that the game itself notes "was never meant to leave the cradle." This boss feels like
a revelation: a machine built for a purpose beyond normal combat, now fully engaged.

BOSS-GRADE VISUAL TREATMENT (art-bible §4.4 / §5.4):
- Significantly larger mass than any common enemy
- Steady radiant Volt cyan glow along the model edge (Boss-Grade ~60% opacity glow)
- The Storm Lance WEAPON is visually the most dramatic element on the screen
- All three break regions must be distinct targetable silhouette zones
- The "crackling with stormlight" concept means ambient electrical arc effects between
  structural members (part of the silhouette — energized structural beams)

SILHOUETTE DESCRIPTION:
Tall, commanding, architecturally complex body plan. Boltwell structure philosophy
taken to boss scale — the exposed beams, struts, and cabling are now monumental. The
overall silhouette is tall and vertical (command unit reads as upright and authoritative)
with complex internal structural geometry visible through deliberate voids.

Key elements:
- WEAPON (region_fraction 40%): The Storm Lance — the most prominent visual element
  and the primary break target. A massive forward-pointing lance/spear weapon extending
  from one ARM at a forward-horizontal angle (ranged attack = forward-horizontal per
  art-bible). The lance head should be architecturally complex: a Boltwell-style
  exposed-structure energy accumulator/emitter that ends in a focused point. Active
  electrical arcing visible along the lance body. This is the thing the player WANTS
  to break and harvest.
- CORE (region_fraction 30%): The CORE housing in the CHASSIS is a prominent reinforced
  chamber — Boltwell exposes structure, so the CORE housing reads as an ornate structural
  cathedral node at the center of the chest, visible through the surrounding structural
  voids. Accessing it means breaking through the chest structure.
- HEAD (region_fraction 22%): Elevated command sensor array — the tallest point of
  the model (elevated above even a tall CHASSIS). A wide horizontal scan array with
  multiple sensor nodes, elevated like an antenna tower. More elaborate than the
  Volt Sentinel's HEAD — this is a command unit with full-spectrum scanning.

BOLTWELL MANUFACTURER FINISH AT BOSS SCALE: The Boltwell structural vocabulary
amplified — architectural in scale, not just decorative. Beams that are structural
members (not just surface detail). Deliberate voids between them showing internal
machinery. Electrical conduit bundles thick as cables, locking rings the size of
industrial joints. The whole machine looks like a precision instrument the size
of a building. Crackling arc energy bridges between structural members.

VOLT ELEMENT ACCENT: Cyan #2FE8E8 DOMINANT throughout — this is the most Volt-heavy
design in the game. Steady radiant boss-grade glow along the entire model edge.
Lightning Fork glyph on the Storm Lance head. Electrical arcing between structural
beam ends (bright cyan energy bridges). All energy conduits glow cyan. HEAD scan
array has cyan sensor glow. The Storm Lance emits a sustained cyan charge.

IDLE STANCE: Upright, commanding. Storm Lance extended forward and raised slightly
(active charge position — the weapon is always ready). HEAD scan array elevated.
CORE housing visible and glowing through the structural voids. The stance reads:
"I was built to end this." Not aggressive — authoritative.

WORLD PALETTE BASE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 structural frame; Circuit Teal #2B6E68
on secondary cabling and coolant conduits; Bone White on primary structural load-bearing
nodes. Cyan #2FE8E8 on all energy systems — the dominant color accent.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64 greyscale — STORM LANCE (forward-pointing dominant weapon),
HEAD (elevated antenna-tower scan array), and CHASSIS with visible CORE HOUSING
(structural cathedral node) must each read as distinct zones. Boss must be unmistakably
larger and more architecturally complex than the Volt Sentinel.

NEGATIVE: No salvage/scrapjaw quality. No organic weathering. No exposed glow sphere
(CORE is visible as a structural node, not a floating orb). No facial expressions.
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. Must be visually MUCH larger and more imposing
than the Volt Sentinel. No rounded UI.
