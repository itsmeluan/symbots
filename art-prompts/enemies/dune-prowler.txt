Enemy Symbot: Dune Prowler. Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 side-view combat perspective.

CONCEPT: "A low-slung raider built for the open flats. It circles, then lunges for
the core." Fast, predatory, low body plan. Like a hunting animal translated into a
Scrapjaw machine — wide, low, and built to close distance and rip into the enemy's
core housing.

SILHOUETTE DESCRIPTION:
Low center of mass body plan — the CHASSIS torso sits close to the ground. LEGS spread
wide and low in a predatory crouch (think: spider or crouched predator — NOT upright
biped). ARM region extends forward and outward with raking-claw geometry — built for
core-exposure attacks. HEAD is a narrow forward-angled sensor cluster, low and predatory
in position (still distinct and elevated above the crouch body, but pressed low and
angled forward — like a hunting posture head). The WEAPON extension from the ARM reaches
forward at a raking angle (claws/hooks, angled down-and-inward per art-bible WEAPON
angle = melee = down-and-inward). The bot reads: "fast, low, and lunging."

SCRAPJAW MANUFACTURER FINISH: Asymmetric panel edges, diagonal slash panel-lines.
Directional surface lines that look mid-motion even at rest (as if the bot is always
in mid-prowl). Quick-release clasps on ARM attachment. Surfaces show desert/sand weathering
— Alloy Ochre #C49A35 abrasion on lower surfaces, scavenged parts with mismatched
surface textures. Sand caught in panel seams implies outdoor terrain.

KINETIC ELEMENT ACCENT: Silver-shift #D8DDE6 on claw tips and ARM joint. Impact Ring
glyph (concentric circles) on the ARM or CHASSIS. Polished chrome on claw impact surfaces.

BREAK REGIONS (distinct targetable silhouette zones):
- ARM (region_fraction 32%): the raking claw/arm extends clearly outward-forward from
  the body. It is the most prominent frontal feature. Breaking it removes the forward
  claw reach from the silhouette.
- CORE (region_fraction 22%): "core_exposed" break event — a visible internal housing
  or panel gap in the CHASSIS center that implies where the CORE would be. Breaking
  this region should look like the CHASSIS chest panel has been cracked open. It is a
  mid-zone feature, not as prominent as the ARM.

IDLE STANCE: Low predatory crouch, claw-arm raised and angled forward (ready to rake),
legs in a wide spread, HEAD sensor angled forward and low. Communicates: "about to lunge."

WORLD PALETTE BASE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 chassis; Alloy Ochre #C49A35 heavy sand
weathering; Harvest Crimson #B33020 Scrapjaw faction detail; Bone White on claw edges.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64 greyscale — ARM (forward claw), CHASSIS with CORE region
(chest panel gap), and LOW LEGS (wide crouch spread) must read as three distinct zones.

NEGATIVE: No upright stance (this bot is always low). No clean factory surfaces. No
visible CORE sphere glowing (the CORE is exposed by damage — implied cavity, not a
glowing orb). No facial expressions. No photoreal. No painterly gradients.
