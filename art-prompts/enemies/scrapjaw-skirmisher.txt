Enemy Symbot: Scrapjaw Skirmisher. Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 side-view combat perspective.

SILHOUETTE DESCRIPTION:
A Scrapjaw-faction raider built for speed and close-quarters bashing. Bipedal, forward-
leaning aggressive stance. Fast-looking body plan: narrow swept CHASSIS torso angled
forward; LEGS are wide-spaced and slightly digitigrade (raised heel, coiled-for-sprint
look); a dominant WEAPON arm — a large improvised hammer (the breakable WEAPON region)
that is the widest element at the end of the ARM extension, reaching forward toward the
target; a compact HEAD sensor crest on top with horizontal visor band.

The bot reads "leads with its hammer and never stops moving" — the WEAPON is the most
visually prominent element (it takes 30% of break region) and extends forward toward
the enemy. LEGS are the second most visible element (wide stance for speed, 20% break).

SCRAPJAW MANUFACTURER FINISH: Diagonal ~45-degree slash panel-lines across CHASSIS and
WEAPON. Quick-release clasps on WEAPON attachment. Asymmetric wear marks. Mixed metal
gauges — some parts thick-plate, some thin-strip. Looks like the hammer was bolted on
from salvage, not designed as a unit. Aggressive, mid-motion-at-rest quality.

KINETIC ELEMENT ACCENT: Silver-shift on the hammer head (polished, chrome impact surface).
Impact Ring glyph (concentric circles) on the hammer face or CHASSIS. Bone White
structural highlights on exposed frame.

BREAK REGIONS (distinct targetable silhouette zones):
- WEAPON (region_fraction 30%): the hammer arm must extend clearly outward/forward
  from the bot body. It is the forward-most element. Breaking it removes the large
  hammer shape from the outline — immediately readable.
- LEG (region_fraction 20%): the LEGS are wide-spread; breaking them should visually
  collapse or remove the wide-stance foundation.

IDLE STANCE: Weight forward, hammer arm raised and slightly back (coiled to swing),
LEGS in a ready sprint position. HEAD sensor tilted toward target. Aggressive energy.

WORLD PALETTE BASE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 chassis; Harvest Crimson #B33020 faction
accent marks; Alloy Ochre #C49A35 weathered metal areas; Bone White on impact surfaces.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64 greyscale — the hammer WEAPON, LEGS (wide stance), and HEAD
(sensor crest elevated) must read as three separate zones by position and shape.

NEGATIVE: No clean factory finish. No symmetrical arms (one dominant hammer arm).
No visible CORE sphere. No facial expressions. No human features. No photoreal.
No painterly gradients. No rounded UI elements.
