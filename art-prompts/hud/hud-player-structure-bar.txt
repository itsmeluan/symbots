HUD element: Player Structure Bar. Canvas 256x32 px. Flat UI, 9-slice stretchable.

A horizontal resource bar showing the player bot's current Structure (hit points).
This is combat-critical UI — always legible, always visible during battle.

ANATOMY (from art-bible §7.1 and battle-visual-design.md §2.2):
The bar is a single 256x32 sprite intended to be 9-sliced in the game engine.
It has three visual layers:

1. CONTAINER/TRACK (full width):
   - Chamfered 45-degree corners at left and right ends (NOT rounded — chamfer
     is the UI signature; corner cuts at ~6px diagonal)
   - Background: C-2 Mid Panel #2C3340 — the "empty" track color
   - Outline: C-5 Divider/Chamfer #4B5668, 1-2px
   - No rounded corners anywhere on this element

2. FILL AREA (the bar fill — this sprite shows the "full" state):
   - Fill color NORMAL: Heal Green #3AB54A
   - The fill occupies the inner area with ~3px inset from the container outline
   - The fill has a subtle top highlight (1px lighter green stripe at top of fill,
     reading as top-lit from the workshop-light metaphor) — but the fill is still
     predominantly flat/matte

3. LABEL AREA INDICATOR:
   - At the left side of the bar, outside the fill: a small LABEL ZONE implied by
     a slightly wider left cap area where a "STRUCTURE" or ♦ icon would be placed
     by the UI programmer. Do NOT bake text into the sprite.
   - Right cap area similarly left clear for the numeric readout (UI programmer places)

STATES NEEDED (generate 4 variants at 256x32):
- Normal (green fill, full): #3AB54A fill
- Critical (red fill ≤20%): #CC3020 Danger Red fill — the fill color changes at threshold
  (generate the full-bar version in red; the engine crops to the correct fill level)
- Empty (no fill): C-2 track only, no fill visible; same container shape
- For this sprite sheet: stack vertically or generate separately as labeled variants

CRITICAL STATE: At critical threshold, a cracked-icon overlay (separate asset) appears.
This bar sprite does NOT include the cracked-icon — that is a separate TextureRect overlay.

9-SLICE NOTE: The center horizontal stretch zone should be featureless fill/track —
the chamfered end caps are the detailed elements. Keep the center stretch-clean.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32px height the bar track vs. fill vs. outline reads as three
distinct layers without color.

NEGATIVE: No rounded corners. No decorative effects that would tile incorrectly under
9-slice stretch. No HUD Dark #1E2229 fill (that reads as empty — use it only for track).
No glow effects on the bar itself (glow = rarity indicator for parts, not HUD bars).
No organic curves. No photoreal.
