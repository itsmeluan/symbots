HUD element: Player Energy Bar. Canvas 256x32 px. Flat UI, 9-slice stretchable.

The player's Energy resource bar. Energy is spent to use moves in combat.
Per battle-visual-design.md §2.2: fill color is Info Blue #4090CC (C-4).

CRITICAL COLOR NOTE (art-bible §4.3 and §4.5):
The Energy bar uses Info Blue #4090CC — NOT Volt Cyan #2FE8E8. This is deliberate:
Info Blue is hue 210°, Volt Cyan is hue 180°. They must not be confused. The Energy
bar is CHROME (it tracks a resource) — it does not signal the Volt ELEMENT. A player
with Volt-element parts will see the Lightning Fork glyph on their moves; the bar
color is orthogonal to element identity. This distinction must be maintained in the
sprite's fill color.

ANATOMY:
- Container: chamfered 45-degree corners (NOT rounded), C-2 #2C3340 empty track,
  C-5 #4B5668 outline, same geometry as the Structure bars
- Fill: Info Blue #4090CC
- Subtle top highlight: 1px lighter blue at the top of the fill (same treatment
  as Structure bar for consistency)
- Label zone clear (left or right cap) — UI programmer places "ENERGY" label

EMPTY STATE: When Energy = 0, all moves with energy cost become greyed-out in
the move panel. The bar shows the empty track only. No special indicator on the
bar itself at zero (the grey-out of moves carries the "can't act" signal).

STATES (generate 2 variants at 256x32):
- Normal Info Blue fill: #4090CC
- Empty track: C-2 #2C3340 only

The Energy bar does NOT have a "critical" state the same way Structure does —
there is no sub-20% danger threshold for Energy in the base system.

9-SLICE MARGINS: Left 8px, Right 8px, Top 4px, Bottom 4px.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32px height — the track/fill/outline three-layer read holds
in greyscale. The bar must be distinguishable from the Structure bar by COLOR only
(same geometry; different fill hue).

NEGATIVE: NO Volt Cyan #2FE8E8 (use Info Blue #4090CC). No Lightning Fork glyph
on the bar sprite itself. No rounded corners. No organic curves. No decorative
tiling elements in stretch zone. No photoreal.
