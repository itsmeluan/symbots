HUD element: Enemy Structure Bar. Canvas 256x32 px. Flat UI, 9-slice stretchable.

The enemy card's Structure bar. Per battle-visual-design.md §2.2: "Same color
assignments as player Structure bar. The shared semantic makes the tactical read
instant: 'green bar = Structure' is universal, not player-specific."

This is IDENTICAL to the player Structure bar in color and shape, but slightly
narrower at default display size (the game engine scales it) and positioned on
the enemy half of the combat screen.

ANATOMY: Identical to hud-player-structure-bar:
- Chamfered 45-degree corners (NOT rounded)
- C-2 Mid Panel #2C3340 empty track
- C-5 Divider #4B5668 outline
- Normal fill: Heal Green #3AB54A
- Critical fill: Danger Red #CC3020

KEY DIFFERENCE from player bar:
- The enemy bar is displayed on the enemy card (upper-right side of the screen)
- The label zone orientation may be mirrored (right-cap for label, left for numeric)
  but the sprite content is the same — orientation is handled in the game engine
- The enemy bar also shows the BREAK STATE visually: when the enemy's structure
  drops significantly, the bar may display differently. For this sprite, produce
  the standard 3 states (normal/critical/empty) — same as player bar.

STATES (generate 3 variants at 256x32):
- Normal green fill: #3AB54A
- Critical red fill: #CC3020
- Empty track

9-SLICE MARGINS: Left 8px, Right 8px, Top 4px, Bottom 4px (same as player bar).

GREYSCALE TEST: Identical greyscale profile to the player bar. The shared visual
language is intentional — the player reads both bars the same way.

NEGATIVE: Same as player structure bar — no rounded corners, no organic curves,
no glow on the bar, no decorative tiling elements in the stretch zone. No photoreal.
