Consumable icon: Repair Kit. Canvas 64x64 px. Front-facing centered icon.

A mid-tier consumable. A full repair kit — more comprehensive than a Weld Patch.
Should read as a complete field repair toolkit, not a single component.

VISUAL CONCEPT: A compact mechanical repair kit case or kit assembly. The dominant
shape is a small toolbox, hard-case, or kit pouch with clearly visible repair tools:
a wrench or spanner visible at the top, patch plates stacked inside or beside it,
possibly a structural rivet driver. The "kit" read is important — this is multiple
tools in one container.

ICON COMPOSITION (centered, 64x64):
- Primary: a compact rectangular hard-case or kit container (Ironclad-like construction
  — geometric, solid, purposeful; slight chamfered corners on the container itself to
  match the game's machined aesthetic)
- Secondary: a wrench or multi-tool visible either protruding from the open case or
  silhouetted on the face of the case. The wrench/tool read immediately signals "repair."
- Optional: patch plates or a small structural segment beside or behind the case

UI CHROME RULES: Chamfered 45-degree frame corners. Double border + diamond pip
(Rare rarity). No element glow (this consumable has no element affinity).

PALETTE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 case body; Alloy Ochre #C49A35 on worn metal/tool
surfaces; Bone White #F2EDDF on structural highlights and tool highlights. Warm
neutral — no element colors. This is a utility tool, not an elemental item.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32x32px reads as a box-with-tool shape — immediately "kit" rather
than a weapon or electronic component.

NEGATIVE: No element glyphs. No neon colors. No weapon silhouette. No photoreal.
No painterly gradients. No rounded frame corners. No overly complex scene — centered
icon composition only.
