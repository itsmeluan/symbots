Consumable icon: Salvage Beacon. Canvas 64x64 px. Front-facing centered icon.

A scanning/loot-enhancement device. Deployed before engaging an enemy to double the
drop rate multiplier for their loot. The "beacon" reads as: active scan device,
target-marking emitter, field intelligence tool.

VISUAL CONCEPT: A compact deployable beacon device — a box or stake with an
active upward-pointing scan emitter or signal horn on top. The beacon is broadcasting
a scan signal (reading enemy loot potential). Should feel like a military/field
intelligence device: sophisticated, precision-built.

ICON COMPOSITION (centered, 64x64):
- Primary: a compact rectangular base unit (Boltwell instrument-grade quality —
  this is a sophisticated device). Angular, chamfered body. Could be handheld or
  stand-mountable.
- Emitter top: a small horn or cone emitter at the top pointing upward, with a
  visible signal pulse wave (concentric arc lines above the emitter tip — the "scan"
  broadcasting upward). The signal arcs are the active element.
- Signal arcs: 2-3 fine concentric arc lines above the emitter (radio/scan signal
  visual). These are the most visually distinctive feature.
- Small screen or indicator on the face showing a target/scan readout

COLOR: Reward Gold #E8B820 on the emitter tip and signal arc lines (this item
creates a loot reward — Gold is the semantic reward color from art-bible §4.3,
with star/trophy glyph). The signal arcs are gold-tinted to read as "valuable
target marked." The body is Slate Gunmetal #374350.

UI CHROME RULES: Chamfered 45-degree frame corners. Double border + diamond pip
(Rare). Soft ambient gold glow ~25% from the emitter.

PALETTE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 device body; Reward Gold #E8B820 on emitter and
signal arcs; Bone White #F2EDDF on structural frame highlights.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32x32px reads as a box-with-emitter-top shape. The signal arcs
above the emitter are the key identifying feature.

NEGATIVE: No Lightning Fork (not Volt). No Flame Chevron. No weapon silhouette.
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded frame corners.
