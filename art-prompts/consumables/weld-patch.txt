Consumable icon: Weld Patch. Canvas 64x64 px. Front-facing centered icon.

A small consumable item icon for the inventory/battle UI. This is the simplest,
cheapest Structure-repair item in the game — a disposable field patch used to
seal cracks and restore minor structural integrity.

VISUAL CONCEPT: A welded metal patch — a small rectangular or irregular-shape metal
plate with fresh weld bead along its edges, designed to be slapped over a crack.
Think: the simplest possible emergency repair tool. A flat metal patch with a
visible weld bead perimeter, plus a small propane/weld-pen applicator beside it
or shown as a secondary element. The patch plate itself is the dominant shape.

ICON COMPOSITION (centered, 64x64):
- Primary: the metal patch plate — slightly irregular rectangular shape, Slate
  Gunmetal #374350 metal surface, Bone White structural highlight on the face
- Secondary: fresh weld bead along the patch perimeter — bright white-orange weld
  point (not Thermal glow — just the physics of welding, a mechanical process)
- Optional: tiny weld pen/applicator at one corner for context

UI CHROME RULES: The icon frame uses chamfered 45-degree corners (NOT rounded).
Single #374350 gunmetal border (Common rarity). No glow.

PALETTE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 patch body; Bone White #F2EDDF structural highlight;
warm white-orange weld bead (hot metal color, kept neutral/matte — not Thermal amber
glyph — this is a physical process, not an element effect). Alloy Ochre #C49A35 as
metal surface variation.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32x32px reads as a flat patch plate with edge detail. Clear and
readable as a "repair material" item.

NEGATIVE: No organic shapes. No element glyphs (this is a mechanical tool, not
elemental). No Thermal Flame Chevron (weld ≠ Thermal element). No photoreal.
No painterly gradients. No rounded corners on the frame. No complex scene — single
centered object, icon format.
