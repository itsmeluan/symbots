Consumable icon: Field Forge. Canvas 64x64 px. Front-facing centered icon.

The most powerful repair consumable in the game. A portable forge unit — not just
patches and tools, but a device capable of rebuilding structural components in the
field. Boss-Grade rarity means this item is rare and powerful. Should read as
significantly more impressive than the Repair Kit.

VISUAL CONCEPT: A compact portable forge unit — a self-contained thermal fabrication
device. Think: a dense, heavy-looking portable industrial furnace/forge in a box.
The forge element (heat, fabrication) should be visually dominant. A smelting chamber
or forge anvil shape with active heat indication at the top or face — glowing hot
metal visible through a viewing aperture. It should feel like: "this rebuilds,
not just patches."

ICON COMPOSITION (centered, 64x64):
- Primary: a compact rectangular forge unit (Ironclad-heavy-industrial construction —
  this is a quality built device, not salvage). Dense, heavy-looking form.
- Heat aperture: a horizontal slot or circular viewport on the front face showing
  intense orange-amber heat inside (NOT a Thermal element glyph — this is
  blacksmith heat, a mechanical process)
- A small anvil or forming plate impression on the top face (the forge surface)
- Thick-plate visual weight (this is heavy industrial equipment miniaturized)

UI CHROME RULES: Chamfered 45-degree frame corners. Triple border + star mark
(Boss-Grade). Steady warm glow ~60% from the heat aperture (internal forge heat,
not an element glow — but warm amber tone reinforces the "powerful heat" concept
without being a Thermal element signal; no Flame Chevron glyph).

PALETTE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 forge body; Alloy Ochre #C49A35 on heat-stained
exterior surfaces; intense orange-amber visible through heat aperture (hot metal color,
interior glow only, matte exterior); Bone White on structural frame edges and anvil surface.

RARITY FRAME: Triple border + star. The star indicates Boss-Grade value. Steady
radial glow from the forge device heat (~60% — powerful item visual weight).

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32x32px reads as a compact dense box with a light aperture.
The heaviness and structural mass distinguish it from the lighter Repair Kit.

NEGATIVE: No Thermal Flame Chevron glyph (this is not an elemental item — it is
a physical forge). No organic shapes. No weapon silhouette. No photoreal.
No painterly gradients. No rounded frame corners.
