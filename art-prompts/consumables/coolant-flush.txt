Consumable icon: Coolant Flush. Canvas 64x64 px. Front-facing centered icon.

A field coolant canister — deployed to flush and reduce the bot's internal heat
buildup. Combats the Heat system that threatens overheat. Should read as:
cooling fluid / temperature reduction / pressurized release.

VISUAL CONCEPT: A pressurized canister or spray bottle containing coolant fluid.
The "flush" action implies pressurized release — the canister is a portable pressurized
vessel with a nozzle or trigger, designed for rapid deployment.

ICON COMPOSITION (centered, 64x64):
- Primary: a compact cylindrical canister with a trigger nozzle at the top — reads
  like a pressurized spray can or portable extinguisher, but smaller and more precise
- The canister body is the dominant shape: slightly elongated vertical cylinder with
  a visible pressure indicator or see-through strip showing fluid level (blue-tinted
  fluid visible inside)
- Nozzle at top: a small forward-pointing nozzle with a mist/spray hint at the tip
- Optional: a frost/condensation visual effect on the canister exterior (cold fluid inside)

COLOR: The coolant fluid is a cool blue-teal (Circuit Teal #2B6E68 base, with hints
of cyan — NOT the full Volt cyan #2FE8E8 which is reserved for element identity, but
a desaturated blue-teal that reads "cooling fluid" rather than "Volt element"). The
canister exterior is Slate Gunmetal #374350. No Lightning Fork glyph — this is not
a Volt element item.

UI CHROME RULES: Chamfered 45-degree frame corners. Single #374350 border (Common).
No element glow.

PALETTE: Slate Gunmetal #374350 canister body; Circuit Teal #2B6E68 for fluid color
(muted, not Volt-cyan); Bone White #F2EDDF on pressure indicator and structural details.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 32x32px reads as a canister-with-nozzle shape. The vertical
cylinder form with top nozzle is the essential read.

NEGATIVE: No Lightning Fork glyph (this is not a Volt elemental item). No Volt cyan
#2FE8E8 (use the desaturated Circuit Teal instead). No fire or heat imagery (this
is the opposite). No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded frame corners.
