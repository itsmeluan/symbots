Part icon: Scrap Core (Scrapjaw, Common, CORE slot, Kinetic).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 view or front-facing view, part on transparent background.

IMPORTANT — CORE SLOT RULES (art-bible §3.3 / §5.3):
- CORE is the ONLY fully-organic-contour object in the game — a SPHERE with an internal
  design. No engineered seams. Deliberately unlike every other part.
- CORE is RENDER-INVISIBLE in battle and overworld — only shown in Workshop and UI contexts.
- This icon is for the Workshop inventory/equip screen and the UI catalog ONLY.
- The CORE sphere carries a slow luminous pulse (Workshop animation) but in this icon
  the pulse is captured mid-cycle as a soft ambient inner glow.
- Do NOT apply the usual bio-mechanical organic-contour-plus-engineered-seam formula here —
  the CORE is a smooth sphere, the exception to all part rules.

DESCRIPTION: "A salvaged reactor core. It hums like it's annoyed."
A rough, scavenged sphere — the power core of a Scrapjaw machine. Smooth sphere
exterior (the CORE form is always spherical) with an internal design visible through
translucent sections or surface-mounted indicator elements. The "salvaged" quality shows
in the surface treatment: weathered, repaired, some Scrapjaw weld patches on the sphere
exterior. The internal design through translucent panels shows the Kinetic elemental
identity (silver-shift energy patterns inside).

SPHERE WITH INTERNAL DESIGN:
- Outer form: smooth sphere, slightly weathered (Scrapjaw origin — weld patches, dents)
- Internal design visible through: translucent panel windows or crystal apertures in
  the sphere surface showing inner energy pattern
- Inner pattern (Kinetic): silver-shift radial lines or concentric geometric pattern,
  the Impact Ring motif echoed at sphere scale
- Slow ambient inner glow (Kinetic silver — captured as a still mid-pulse)

SCRAPJAW CHARACTER ON A SPHERE: Minimal surface markings (the sphere form is sacrosanct)
but some Scrapjaw quick-release latch nodes at cardinal points (the attachment grammar
must be present even on the CORE). Small diagonal slash marks in weld-patch areas.

RARITY FRAME (Common): Single #374350 border. No ambient glow (the inner glow is the
CORE's own life — separate from rarity glow). Plain frame.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px reads unmistakably as a sphere (the game's ONLY sphere form).
Distinguishable from all other part shapes by its rounded, globe-like silhouette.

NEGATIVE: Do NOT make this look like a battle model component (it is Workshop/UI only).
Do NOT apply engineered panel-line seams across the sphere exterior (the sphere is smooth).
No forward-pointing weapon shape. No limb shape. No photoreal. No painterly gradients.
Do NOT show this in a battle or overworld context.
