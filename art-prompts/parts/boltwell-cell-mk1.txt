Part icon: Cell Mk1 (Boltwell, Common, ENERGY_CELL slot, Volt).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 or front view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Standard-issue energy cell. Does the one job."
A power cell module — compact, rectangular, functional. This is what powers the energy
systems of a Boltwell bot. Should look like an industrial battery or capacitor bank:
a rectangular housing with connector terminals and charge indicator surfaces.

ENERGY_CELL SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- Does NOT produce distinct silhouette protrusions in battle — flush-embedded
- Compact rectangular form — a "brick" of stored energy
- Visible as a small flush indicator panel within the CHASSIS zone at battle scale
- At Workshop scale this icon reads clearly as an energy storage unit

VISUAL CHARACTER:
Rectangular power cell housing. Prominent connector terminals at one end (where it
connects to the bot's power bus — Boltwell locking ring connectors). Charge level
indicator surface (a row of segmented indicator cells or a gauge window showing
stored energy state). Structural housing has Boltwell cable-tie retention details
on the exterior for chassis mounting. The form is somewhere between an industrial
battery and a high-tech capacitor module.

BOLTWELL FINISH: Exposed structural detail at module scale — visible internal-facing
conduit ports, locking connector ring terminals, retention clip geometry on housing
edges. High-tech instrument precision. No organic curves anywhere.

VOLT ELEMENT: Cyan #2FE8E8 on the charge indicator surface and connector terminals.
Lightning Fork glyph on the cell face or indicator panel. Inner stored-energy glow
visible through the gauge window in cyan.

RARITY FRAME (Common): Single #374350 border. No glow. Standard-issue feel.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px reads as a compact rectangular power module with visible
terminals and a charge indicator. Distinct from the Logic Chip (CHIPSET) by the
rectangular "brick" form and terminal geometry.

NEGATIVE: No limb shape. No weapon shape. No sphere (that is CORE). No Scrapjaw
diagonal lines. No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
