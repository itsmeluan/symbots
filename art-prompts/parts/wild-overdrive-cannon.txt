Part icon: Overdrive Cannon (Wild, Prototype, WEAPON slot, Thermal).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 or side view angled forward-horizontal, part isolated on
transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Jury-rigged to overload. Melts targets and itself."
A wild-origin Prototype ranged weapon of enormous destructive output. This cannon was
never meant to sustain the output it delivers — it is perpetually on the edge of
catastrophic self-destruction. The "jury-rigged to overload" concept must be readable
on the form: a cannon that looks like it has been pushed past its design limits and held
together with whatever was at hand, while producing devastating Thermal output.

Prototype rarity = the rarest item tier in the game. This weapon must look dangerous
and unstable — the power inside is barely contained.

WEAPON SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- Ranged WEAPON: forward-horizontal angle (the cannon barrel points at the target)
- Must "point" — the business end (cannon mouth) is the forward element
- The direction of threat reads at 64x64px

WILD MANUFACTURER FINISH ON A PROTOTYPE WEAPON:
The base form is organic-wild: the cannon body has grown-or-adapted contour, not a
machined barrel. Think of a heavy cannon barrel that has organic mineral or shell-like
accretions on its exterior — the weapon grew around an impossibly powerful core.
The wild seams and attachment nodes are still engineered (mount bracket is mechanical),
but the cannon body itself has an evolved-organic quality: irregular thickening along
the barrel, crystalline or organic extrusions at the high-heat zones where it's been
pushed past limits.

JURY-RIGGED OVERLOAD CHARACTER:
Visible emergency reinforcement: clamps and hasps added to the barrel to keep it from
splitting (Scrapjaw-adjacent salvage additions to a wild base — cross-manufacturer
field repair). Scorch marks and heat warping around the barrel exit. Cracks in the
organic barrel surface sealed with emergency welds. The weapon has been repaired
multiple times. Something is leaking amber/orange thermal energy from the hairline cracks.

THERMAL ELEMENT (Prototype): Amber #F0900A dominant on all heat-discharge and crack-glow
areas. Flame Chevron glyph (upward-stacked V shapes) on the barrel face or side.
Heavy emissive amber bloom from every crack and vent — the thermal output is barely
contained. The Prototype shimmer (chromatic aberration/shimmer at <3 Hz) is captured
in this still as a slight color-aberration halo around the barrel at maximum-heat areas
(warm amber bleeding to red-orange at edges — captured mid-flicker).

PROTOTYPE RARITY FRAME: Irregular/animated border treatment (in still: a slightly
uneven glowing border rather than a clean geometric one) + warning glyph mark.
Chromatic shimmer captured as slight color fringe. The frame itself looks unstable.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px — the forward-pointing cannon barrel with the widened/
dangerous cannon mouth reads as a ranged weapon. The irregular surface texture
distinguishes it from the precision Boltwell weapons.

NEGATIVE: No clean precision barrel (this is wild and jury-rigged). No Ironclad or
Boltwell manufacturer finish. No melee angle. No stable-looking weapon (it should look
dangerous to operate). No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
