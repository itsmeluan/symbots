Part icon: Logic Chip (Boltwell, Common, CHIPSET slot, Volt).
Canvas 256x256 px. Front/top-down view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Boltwell silicon. Runs cool, thinks fast enough."
A compact electronic module — the CHIPSET slot part. This is embedded INSIDE the
CHASSIS (flush-mounted, no silhouette protrusion in battle). For the icon, it should
look like an actual high-tech circuit board or processing module: small, dense, precise.

CHIPSET SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- Does NOT protrude from the bot silhouette — it is flush-embedded
- Small, compact, symmetric indicator panel form
- At battle scale (64px) it may be below the resolution threshold (correct)
- At Workshop/catalog scale (this icon) it reads as fine detail rewarding scrutiny
- NEVER designed with geometry that could be confused with ARMS or WEAPON

VISUAL CHARACTER:
A circuit board / processing module aesthetic. Compact rectangular or square form.
Dense surface detail: microprocessor pads, trace lines, component mounts visible at
Workshop zoom scale. The Boltwell high-tech instrument finish at miniaturized scale —
cable tie mounting posts, locking retention clips on the edge for chassis mounting.
Small indicator lights or trace-connections at corners (circuit board / instrument panel).

BOLTWELL FINISH: Exposed structure at this scale means visible circuit trace geometry
(trace runs as structural detail), connector post rows, locking clip edges.
Instrument-grade precision manufacturing — clean, purposeful, no wasted surface.

VOLT ELEMENT: Cyan #2FE8E8 on circuit trace highlights and indicator pads. Lightning
Fork glyph (bifurcated diagonal bolt) on the primary processor area.

RARITY FRAME (Common): Single #374350 border. No glow. Entry-tier processing part.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px reads as a small electronic module/chip — dense detail,
rectangular, clearly electronic rather than structural.

NEGATIVE: No limb shape. No weapon shape. No body/chassis protrusion. No organic curves.
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
