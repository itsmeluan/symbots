Part icon: Bash Hammer (Scrapjaw, Common, WEAPON slot, Kinetic).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "A pipe with ambition. Hits things until they stop."
The simplest melee weapon in the game. This should look like exactly what it is:
an oversized mechanical impact tool — a heavy hammer or wrecking-maul made from
repurposed industrial parts. It does not need to be elegant. It needs to look like
it hurts.

WEAPON SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- WEAPON is the only slot that extends FORWARD
- Melee WEAPON: angled down-and-inward (the angle of a strike)
- Must have a clear "pointing" direction — the business end (hammer head) is the forward/
  active end; the mount end (grip/handle) attaches to the ARM slot
- At 64x64px the weapon must be the part that "points" — direction of threat reads

VISUAL CHARACTER:
Heavy hammer head at the forward end — a blocky, thick rectangular or cylindrical head
made from a repurposed industrial part (a chunk of machinery welded to a handle).
Handle/shaft connecting to mount end — straight, thick enough to be credible as the
arm that swings this. The hammer head has dents and impact marks (it has hit things
before). Simple silhouette: shaft + head. The head is wider than the shaft.

SCRAPJAW FINISH: Diagonal slash panel-lines across the hammer head. Weld mark where
the head was attached to the handle (salvaged assembly — the head and handle were
separate pieces). Quick-release mount at the handle end.

KINETIC ELEMENT: Silver-shift #D8DDE6 on the hammer head impact face (polished from
use — chrome Impact Ring finish on the striking surface). Impact Ring glyph on the
hammer face. Bone White structural highlights on the handle edges.

RARITY FRAME (Common): Single #374350 gunmetal border. No glow. Plain and functional.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px — wide hammer head at one end, narrower shaft at the other.
The "heavy end" direction is unambiguous.

NEGATIVE: No energy glow unrelated to Kinetic element. No complex mechanical details
(this is a pipe with ambition — keep it simple). No Boltwell or Ironclad finish.
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
