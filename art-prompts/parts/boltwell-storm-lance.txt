Part icon: Storm Lance (Boltwell, Boss-Grade, WEAPON slot, Volt).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 or side view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Boltwell's flagship arc-driver, torn from the Warden's cradle. It answers only to a storm."
This is the Storm Warden boss's weapon, harvested. The most powerful ranged WEAPON in
the current roster (Boss-Grade). It should look exactly like what it is: a weapon
of extraordinary precision and power that came from a command unit. Not oversized brute
force — extreme technical sophistication. The Storm Lance is the "flagship" weapon.

WEAPON SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE: Ranged WEAPON — forward-horizontal angle. The business
end (emitter/lance tip) is forward. This is named a "lance" — it has the elongated
forward-projecting form of a lance, but electrically charged. Not a blunt projector
(that is the Arc Blaster) — the Storm Lance is long, precise, and focused.

VISUAL CHARACTER:
An elongated, architecturally complex ranged weapon. The form is lance-like:
- SHAFT: long, slender structural beam with Boltwell cable routing and connector
  rings along its length (structural members visible, not enclosed)
- EMITTER TIP: the forward end narrows to a precision emitter point surrounded by
  arc-focusing rings (like a particle accelerator convergence point — the energy
  concentrates to a point before discharge). Multiple focusing rings arrayed along
  the forward 1/3 of the weapon, tightening toward the tip.
- MOUNT END: rear-mounted connection bracket with Boltwell locking rings for
  ARM attachment. The connection is substantial — this weapon has mass and recoil.
- ACTIVE ARC: electrical arcing visible between the focusing rings (the weapon is
  always slightly charged — it is never truly off)

BOLTWELL FINISH AMPLIFIED TO BOSS QUALITY: All Boltwell structural vocabulary, but
executed with the precision of a flagship product. Cable routing is immaculate —
no cable ties that look improvised, every connection is deliberate. Structural
members in precisely machined cross-sections. The scorch marks from discharge are
minimal (this weapon is too refined to leave much residue).

VOLT ELEMENT (Boss-Grade): Cyan #2FE8E8 DOMINANT — the brightest Volt expression.
Steady radiant boss-grade glow (~60%) along the entire weapon edge. Lightning Fork
glyph at the emitter tip (large, prominent). Active arcing between focusing rings
in cyan. The weapon reads: "sustained stormlight."

RARITY FRAME (Boss-Grade): Triple border + star mark. Steady radiant cyan glow ~60%.
Hard edge highlight at the outer border.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px — the elongated forward-pointing lance form with focusing
rings along its length reads as a precision ranged weapon. Distinct from the Arc Blaster
(which is wider/chunkier) — this is long, precise, focused.

NEGATIVE: No blunt/hammer form (this is a lance, not a club). No Scrapjaw salvage
quality. No closed housing (Boltwell structural design is always partially open).
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. Must look more refined than the Arc Blaster.
No rounded UI frame.
