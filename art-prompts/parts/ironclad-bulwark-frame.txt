Part icon: Bulwark Frame (Ironclad, Common, CHASSIS slot, Thermal, Defense archetype).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 front view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Ironclad's entry-tier plating. Overbuilt on purpose."
The defense CHASSIS. This is the torso/body plate of a bot — the widest, most central
component that everything else attaches to. For the defense archetype, this reads as:
wide, convex, blocklike, armored wall.

CHASSIS SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- Always the single widest element in the mid-zone
- Outer edge defines "shoulder width" — the reference boundary for all other parts
- Defense archetype: wide, convex, blocklike with chamfered (45-degree cut) corners
- Panel-line anchor: all other mid-zone parts' panel lines would continue from this
- CORE socket is implied at center (for CORE slot insertion — an internal cavity or
  locking ring visible at center front)
- ARMS attachment points visible on the lateral edges (the shoulder seam nodes)

IRONCLAD FINISH: Dense perpendicular grid panel-lines across the entire front face.
Prominent hex bolts at every panel corner (4+ visible bolts on the face). Planar,
faceted surfaces — the convex curve of defense armor expressed through flat planes
chamfered together (not a smooth organic curve — geometric facets that approximate a
convex shell). Thick-plate visual weight. Heavy-industrial register.

THERMAL ELEMENT: Amber #F0900A as subtle accent on panel borders and the central
CORE socket ring. Flame Chevron glyph (upward-stacked V shapes) on the central chest
panel. Entry-level Thermal presence — muted because this is Common rarity.

RARITY FRAME (Common): Single #374350 border. No glow. Entry-tier look.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px reads as a wide torso/chest plate with a clear central
socket and visible lateral attachment points for ARMS. Widest part in any assembled bot.

NEGATIVE: No legs shape. No head shape. No arm extension. No CORE sphere (the socket
is an empty ring — CORE is a separate part). No Scrapjaw diagonal lines. No Boltwell
struts. No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
