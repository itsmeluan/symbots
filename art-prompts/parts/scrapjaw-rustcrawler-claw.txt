Part icon: Rustcrawler Claw (Scrapjaw, Boss-Grade, ARMS slot, Kinetic).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Torn from the Rustcrawler boss. Still twitches for a grip."
This is the Rust Tyrant boss's crusher claw, harvested as a part. It is a raw,
powerful, unsettling arm — it looks like it was recently detached from the boss's
body (some Scrapjaw-style breakage at the mount end, not a clean cut). The claw
itself is large, aggressive, multi-tined. It should look like it wants to grab things.
"Still twitches" implies mechanical residual tension in the claw joints.

VISUAL CHARACTER:
The dominant shape is the CLAW — a large multi-fingered or multi-tined grasping
mechanism, chrome-plished on the grip surfaces (the most dangerous parts shine),
with Scrapjaw salvage character at the mount end (where it was torn, not cleanly
detached). The arm/forearm section between mount and claw is thick and powerful.
Unlike the Servo Arm this is not a precision tool — it is a weapon-arm that crushes.

SCRAPJAW FINISH: Asymmetric claw tines (not perfectly symmetric — one tine slightly
larger, one slightly bent from prior impacts). Diagonal slash panel-lines on the forearm.
The mount end shows stress fractures and torn edges (boss-torn origin). Quick-release
clasps present but look like they barely hold the power inside.

KINETIC ELEMENT: Polished chrome #D8DDE6 on the claw tine faces (the impact surfaces
— shiniest elements in the design, Kinetic chrome finish per art-bible). Impact Ring
glyph (concentric circles) on the palm/claw center. Silver-shift metallic finish on
the forearm. Boss-Grade steady radiant silver glow around the entire part edge.

RARITY FRAME (Boss-Grade): Triple border (three concentric border lines) + star mark
in corner. Steady radiant silver-toned glow (~60% opacity) around the part silhouette.
Hard edge highlight where the glow meets the part outline.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px — reads as an arm with a large claw at one end. The claw
tines must be distinguishable as the forward/active end.

NEGATIVE: No clean detachment point (this was torn off). No symmetric tines. No factory
polish on the mount end. No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
