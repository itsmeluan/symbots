Part icon: Aegis Frame (Ironclad, Rare, CHASSIS slot, Thermal, Defense archetype).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 front view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Ironclad's tournament-grade shell. Sheds a plasma lance like rain."
The upgraded defense CHASSIS. Same Ironclad-defense archetype as the Bulwark Frame
but with significantly higher quality: tournament-grade surface treatment, ablative
plating panels visible on the outer face, more elaborate panel-line geometry.

VISUAL DIFFERENTIATION FROM BULWARK FRAME:
- Ablative plating panels: visible layered angled surface tiles on the outer armor face
  (like ablative armor — plates angled to deflect, not just absorb). These look like
  deliberate tournament engineering, not just "thicker."
- More elaborate perpendicular grid panel-lines — finer subdivisions, more precise.
- Larger, more prominent hex bolts (tournament-grade fasteners look more machined)
- A more pronounced convex curve to the central chest panel
- The CORE socket is more elaborately framed (reinforced mounting ring, not just an opening)
- The ARMS attachment shoulder seam nodes are more prominent (stronger connection points)

IRONCLAD FINISH: All the Ironclad vocabulary (perpendicular grid, hex bolts, planar faces)
but at a higher precision and density. "Tournament-grade" = the same language, more refined.
Ablative plate geometry adds a secondary angular surface layer over the primary armor.

THERMAL ELEMENT: Amber #F0900A on the ablative plate edges (heat-resistant tile borders)
and on the CORE socket ring. Flame Chevron glyph on the central chest panel, slightly more
elaborate than the Bulwark Frame glyph (Rare tier = more surface detail budget). Soft
ambient amber glow at ~25% (Rare rarity glow — elemental color ambient).

RARITY FRAME (Rare): Double border + diamond pip in corner. Soft ambient amber Thermal
glow around the part shape at ~25% opacity.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px distinguishable from the Bulwark Frame by the visible ablative
plate layering on the outer surface. Reads as "the more complex version of the same form."

NEGATIVE: No Scrapjaw diagonal lines. No Boltwell struts. No organic curves on the
engineering surface. No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
