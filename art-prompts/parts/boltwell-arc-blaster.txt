Part icon: Arc Blaster (Boltwell, Rare, WEAPON slot, Volt).
Canvas 256x256 px. 3/4 or side view, part isolated on transparent background.

DESCRIPTION: "Lobs a fat blue arc. Smells like a storm afterward."
A ranged energy weapon that fires crackling arc charges. The WEAPON slot extends forward —
ranged weapons angle forward-horizontal per art-bible. This one lobs arcs (not a laser
beam — a fat arcing projectile launcher).

WEAPON SLOT SHAPE LANGUAGE (art-bible §3.3):
- WEAPON is the ONLY slot that extends FORWARD
- Ranged WEAPON: angled forward-horizontal (pointing at target)
- The direction of threat must read at 64x64px
- The weapon must "point" — the business end (emitter/barrel) is forward-facing
- Mount end attaches to ARM slot

VISUAL CHARACTER:
A chunky but precision arc discharge weapon. Not a laser — more like an arc-capacitor
projector: a barrel/emitter with a wide discharge opening at the front, capacitor
coils or charge rings along the body, and a sturdy mounting bracket at the rear.
The emitter mouth is wide (it "lobs" arc charges — wide projectile, not narrow beam).
Capacitor coils along the body: visible rings or fins with Boltwell cable routing
between them (each coil charges the arc). Energy crackle visible at the emitter mouth.

BOLTWELL FINISH: Exposed structural capacitor coil mounts (the coils are mounted on
visible structural beams, not enclosed in smooth housing). Cable bundles between coils.
Locking ring connector at the ARM mount. Instrument-grade precision assembly.
Some scorch marks around the emitter mouth (arc discharge residue).

VOLT ELEMENT: Cyan #2FE8E8 dominant at the emitter mouth (active arc energy) and
along the capacitor coil gaps. Lightning Fork glyph at the emitter face. Soft ambient
cyan glow at emitter and coil gaps. Rare glow: soft ambient cyan at ~25% around the part.

RARITY FRAME (Rare): Double border + diamond pip. Soft ambient cyan glow ~25%.

GREYSCALE TEST: At 64x64px — the wide emitter mouth (forward end) and the capacitor
coil body must distinguish this from a melee weapon. Forward-pointing direction reads.

NEGATIVE: No melee/hammer shape. No downward-inward angle (ranged = forward-horizontal).
No smooth closed housing (Boltwell exposes structure). No Scrapjaw diagonal marks.
No photoreal. No painterly gradients. No rounded UI frame.
