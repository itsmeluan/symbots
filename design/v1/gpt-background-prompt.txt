=== STYLE ===

Mid-90s mecha TV anime. Think Medabots, Digimon, Zoids. Illustrated background art for a game,
NOT concept art, NOT a painting, NOT photorealistic.

- Cel shading: two or three flat tones per surface, hard-edged transitions between them. No soft
  gradients, no airbrushing, no photographic texture, no grain.
- Defined outlines on structures and silhouettes — darker than the fill, not pure black.
- Saturated, confident colour. Every location has its own colour identity.
- Simplified chunky shapes, readable at thumbnail size. Detail comes from silhouette and colour
  blocking, never from surface noise.
- Clear directional light with a stated time of day. Skies can be dramatic: big flat cloud
  shapes, banded gradients, a low sun.

It should look like a frame from an anime, not like a rendering.

=== FORMAT AND COMPOSITION — these rules are strict ===

Every image: 1080 x 1920 pixels, 9:16 portrait, PNG.

Bright cartoon creature sprites get placed on top of these backgrounds by the game, four on the
left and four on the right, standing in two vertical columns. The composition must leave room for
them:

- The HORIZON sits 30% down from the top. Everything below it is a continuous walkable GROUND
  PLANE in one-point perspective, receding to a vanishing point at the centre of the horizon.
  Floor seams and edge lines converge toward that point.
- The ground plane is UNBROKEN and UNOBSTRUCTED from 32% to 82% of the image height. Nothing
  crosses it in that range — no walls, pits, water, railings, crates, machinery or debris.
- Two STANDING LANES stay visually calm and evenly lit: one centred at 24% of the width, one at
  76%, each about 30% of the width across. Flat readable ground only in those two lanes — no
  props, no high-contrast detail, no cast shadows.
- A subtle VERTICAL DIVIDER runs down the centre at 50% width for the full height of the floor —
  a floor seam, a channel, a strip of different material, a line of embedded lights. Subtle, not
  a wall, and it must not block the view.
- The top 7% is empty sky. The bottom 18% is plain darker floor.
- All landmarks and detail live ABOVE the horizon and in the outer margins. Never on the floor.
- Keep the floor a MID value — not black, not white, and never busier than the creatures that
  will stand on it.

Do NOT include: any character, robot, creature, person or animal; any text, numbers, logos or
watermarks; any UI, frame, border or vignette; any foreground object in front of the camera.
