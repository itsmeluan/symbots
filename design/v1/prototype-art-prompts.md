# Prototype Symbots — concepts and art prompts

Five **PROTOTYPE**-rarity species (the top rung: +50% base stats, 4 unique passives,
15 Overclock levels). They must read as *rarer* than the 16 shipped Symbots at a glance,
before any number is shown.

## What makes a Prototype look like a Prototype

The existing roster is **finished machines** — clean silhouettes, sealed panels, a single
clear animal read (turtle, spider, wasp, wolf). Prototypes invert that on purpose:

| Common / Rare / Epic | Prototype |
|---|---|
| Sealed, panelled, symmetrical | **Exposed internals** — visible coolant lines, open frames |
| One material story | **Mismatched** — salvaged plating over precision cores |
| Balanced silhouette | **Asymmetric** — one oversized limb, offset core |
| Solid colour blocks | **A single hot emissive accent** that grows across marks |

They are *field experiments that worked*. Powerful and slightly wrong-looking.

## Rules that apply to EVERY prompt

- Pixel art sprite, single character, **transparent background**, no ground shadow baked in.
- **Facing right** (the player's side faces right; enemies are mirrored in engine).
- Full body, side-three-quarter view, readable at 120 px tall.
- **Mechanical, NOT organic** — no fur, no skin, no eyes-with-pupils. Lenses, vents, actuators.
- Limited palette: 2 base metals + 1 emissive accent. Accent brightens Mk I → Mk III.
- **Size escalation is mandatory**: Mk II clearly larger and busier than Mk I; Mk III
  clearly larger again, with a silhouette change (extra limbs, deployed frame, or a mount).
- Keep the same accent colour and core motif across the three marks so the line reads as
  one creature evolving, not three creatures.

## 1. NULLWRIGHT — the unfinished archivist
**Role:** SUPPORT · **Accent:** pale violet · **Motif:** floating ring segments, no visible joints

- **Mk I** — `Pixel art robot creature, side three-quarter view facing right, transparent background. A small hovering drone the size of a briefcase: a matte grey ceramic core shaped like an open book, held between two broken ring segments that float apart with no visible joint. One pale violet lens where a spine would be. Exposed ribbon cable trailing underneath. Mechanical, no organic parts. Limited palette: grey ceramic, gunmetal, pale violet emissive. Charismatic, Medabots-inspired, clean pixel clusters, readable at 120px.`
- **Mk II** — `Same creature, larger and heavier. The ring segments have multiplied to four and now orbit a taller core with an open frame showing violet-lit internals. Two thin manipulator arms unfold from the sides. Trailing cables bundled into a braid. Violet emissive brighter and now lights the underside. Same palette and motif, clearly bigger and busier than the previous form.`
- **Mk III** — `Same creature, dramatically larger — roughly three times the first form. The rings now form a broken halo wide enough to frame the whole body, some segments cracked and repaired with mismatched salvaged plating. The core has split open into a floating library of stacked plates. Four manipulator arms. Violet emissive intense, casting light on every surface. Imposing, top-heavy, unmistakably an evolved final form.`

## 2. GRAVELOCK — the overbuilt anchor
**Role:** TANK · **Accent:** hot orange · **Motif:** one oversized arm, ratchet teeth

- **Mk I** — `Pixel art robot creature, side three-quarter view facing right, transparent background. A squat quadruped the size of a toolbox, built asymmetrically: its right foreleg is a massive ratcheted clamp twice the size of the other three legs. Dull steel plating over an exposed orange-lit piston core. Blocky, low to the ground. Mechanical, no organic parts. Palette: dull steel, dark iron, hot orange emissive. Charismatic, Medabots-inspired, readable at 120px.`
- **Mk II** — `Same creature, clearly larger and bulkier. The oversized clamp arm now has a second smaller clamp mounted above it, and the rear legs have grown anchor spikes that bite the ground. Chest plating peeled back to show an orange-glowing piston assembly. Heavier stance, wider silhouette. Same palette and asymmetric motif.`
- **Mk III** — `Same creature, enormous — a walking anchor. The clamp arm has become a full siege limb with ratchet teeth along its length, braced by a counterweight frame on the opposite shoulder. Rear legs are hydraulic pylons. Orange emissive glows through gaps in mismatched salvaged armour bolted over the original steel. Dominating, wide, unmistakably a final form.`

## 3. VEXHERTZ — the resonance experiment
**Role:** DPS · **Accent:** acid green · **Motif:** tuning-fork prongs, visible sound rings

- **Mk I** — `Pixel art robot creature, side three-quarter view facing right, transparent background. A lean biped the size of a cat, standing on two thin reverse-jointed legs. Its head is a single tuning-fork prong with an acid green filament between the tines. Narrow chest with an open resonator cavity. Twitchy, unstable posture. Mechanical, no organic parts. Palette: white enamel, gunmetal, acid green emissive. Charismatic, Medabots-inspired, readable at 120px.`
- **Mk II** — `Same creature, taller and more elaborate. Three tuning-fork prongs now crown the head at different lengths, green filaments arcing between them. The resonator cavity in the chest has grown and shows visible ring ripples. Arms end in prong emitters. Clearly larger than the previous form, same palette and prong motif.`
- **Mk III** — `Same creature, towering and elongated — twice the height of the second form. A crown of seven prongs of varying length fans from the head like a broken antenna array, all wired to a chest resonator that has split the torso open with green light. Long thin limbs, one arm replaced entirely by an emitter cone. Acid green emissive blazing. Unsettling, spindly, unmistakably a final form.`

## 4. SUMPCOIL — the drowned reactor
**Role:** HEALER · **Accent:** deep cyan · **Motif:** coiled coolant tubing, weeping vents

- **Mk I** — `Pixel art robot creature, side three-quarter view facing right, transparent background. A rounded low unit the size of a bucket, wrapped in coiled transparent coolant tubing that glows deep cyan. Squat body on three stubby legs, a single wide vent slot for a face, dripping condensation. Corroded copper and pale grey. Mechanical, no organic parts. Palette: corroded copper, pale grey, deep cyan emissive. Charismatic, Medabots-inspired, readable at 120px.`
- **Mk II** — `Same creature, larger and more overgrown with tubing. The coils now wrap a taller body twice over and sprout two tube-fed arms that end in nozzle heads. More vents, more condensation, cyan glow visible through the coolant. Four legs instead of three. Same palette and coil motif, clearly bigger.`
- **Mk III** — `Same creature, massive and reactor-like. A tall barrel core is completely encased in layered coolant coils, some cracked and patched with mismatched salvaged clamps, venting cyan mist from a dozen ports. Four nozzle arms arranged radially. Broad stable base of six short legs. Deep cyan emissive lighting the mist around it. Heavy, imposing, unmistakably a final form.`

## 5. HALFMASK — the twin that did not split
**Role:** DPS · **Accent:** magenta · **Motif:** one face mask, one exposed skull-frame

- **Mk I** — `Pixel art robot creature, side three-quarter view facing right, transparent background. A small humanoid biped the size of a housecat, split down the middle: its left half is smooth finished white plating with a blank mask face, its right half is an exposed magenta-lit frame with no covering at all. Mismatched arms. Mechanical, no organic parts. Palette: white plating, exposed dark frame, magenta emissive. Charismatic, Medabots-inspired, readable at 120px.`
- **Mk II** — `Same creature, clearly larger. The finished half has grown layered armour and a horned mask crest; the exposed half has grown a second magenta-lit arm sprouting from the same shoulder, so the two sides no longer match in limb count. Asymmetry more extreme. Same palette and split motif.`
- **Mk III** — `Same creature, large and disturbing — three times the first form. The finished half is now full ceremonial plating with an ornate mask; the exposed half has become a sprawl of magenta-lit frame with three arms and a trailing spine, dwarfing the finished side. The body leans off-axis from the imbalance. Magenta emissive intense. Unmistakably a final form and unmistakably the same creature.`

## Still to author (needs a design decision)

The `.tres` species files are **not** written yet. Prototype rarity requires **4 unique
passives each** (`UNIQUE_PASSIVES_BY_RARITY`), which is **20 new passive ids** — a content
decision, not a mechanical one. Options:

1. Author 20 new passives (biggest, most distinct).
2. Give Prototypes 4 passives drawn from a smaller pool of new ones shared across the five.
3. Lower the Prototype passive count in the rarity table.

Nothing ships until that is picked, because `species_validator` checks the authored count
against the rarity table.
